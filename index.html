Welcome to MathRun!

What is MathRun?

MathRun is a Roblox game where you have to answer different levels of math problems to get away from the deadly monster!

What is MathRun for?

MathRun teaches and helps kids and players practice how to do quick math like adddition, subtraction, and multiplication. The game is kid friendly and makes learning fun for everyone! Just make sure you don't get caught...
